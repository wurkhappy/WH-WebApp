About Wurk Happy

Wurk Happy is an online platform for freelancers and their clients that combines agreement, invoicing, and payment processing into one simple workflow.

How do you make freelancing easier?

We started asking this question nearly 4 years ago as freedom loving freelancers. Freelancing felt harder than it needed to be. So we started thinking about how there could be a better way to work. We used to spend days crafting contracts that would become useless as soon as the client changed her mind about what kind of work she wanted. Knowing if our clients had the same expectations about what work was to be done and how it was to be deemed complete was very important - especially when it was time to invoice - but constant check-ins were also very time consuming and difficult to document. Billing was never straightfoward. Different clients had different ways to pay. There just wasn't an easy standard.

From these frustrations and the insight of hundreds of freelancers, Wurk Happy was slowly born. From the beginning we set out to build a better process rather than just another tool. In fact, we aimed to be the only real business workflow a freelancer needs - ultra simple to start using, yet powerful enough to cater to all freelance situations.

If you're like us, you crave simplicity. We didn’t appreciate the tools that were out there. We didn’t want to use excel, word, an invoicing app, a payment app, and a project management app just to manage our freelance relationships. We just wanted things to feel simple - so we could enjoy those occasional zen moments that happen in the flow of great work.

As the process came together - agreement, communication, invoicing, and payment, we found that we could make each individual component feel simpler to use because it was part of a deliberate, connected workflow for both the freelancer and client.

We've come a long way towards making a better way to freelance - and we're humbled to be at a point where freelancers really appreciate the work we've put in. But we have a long way to go. Everyday we make Wurk Happy better so we can always provide the best service out there.
