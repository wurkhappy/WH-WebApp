About Wurk Happy

Wurk Happy is an online platform for freelancers and their clients that combines agreement, invoicing, and payment processing into one simple workflow.

How do you make freelancing easier?

We started asking this question nearly 4 years ago. Freelancing is hard and it felt like there had to be a better way to work. We used to spend days crafting contracts that would become useless as soon as the client changed her mind about what kind of work she wanted. Knowing if my client and I had the same expectations about what work was to be done and how it was to be deemed complete was very important, especially when it was time to invoice - yet it was also very time consuming. Billing was never straightfoward. Different clients have different ways to pay. There just wasn't a standard that was incredibly easy.

From these frustrations and the insight of hundreds of freelancers, Wurk Happy was slowly born. From the beginning we set out to build a better process rather than just another tool. In fact, we aimed to be the only real business workflow a freelancer needs - ultra simple to start using yet powerful enough to cater to all freelance situations.

If you're like us, you crave simplicity. I didn’t appreciate the tools that were out there. I didn’t want to use excel, word, an invoicing app, a payment app, and a project management app just to manage my relationship with my client. I justed wanted things to feel simple - so I could enjoy those occasional zen moments that happen in the flow of great work. I also didn’t want to live in software. I wanted software that was there when I needed it to be and invisible when I didn't need it.

As the process came together - agreement, communication, invoicing, and payment, we found that we could make each individual component feel simpler to use because it's part of a process that we guide the freelancer and client through.

We've come a long way in making the a better way to freelance - and we're humbled to be at a point where freelancers really appreicate the work we've put in. But we have a long way to go. Everyday we make Wurk Happy better. We're focused on providing the best service we can.

If you ever have any suggestions, please reach out to us. We would love to hear from you.
