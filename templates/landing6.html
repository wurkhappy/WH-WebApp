How Wurk Happy Works

1. Create and send an agreement in minutes.

Our agreements are simple - just the scope of work and payment schedule - so you and your client can focus on doing great work together.

When you create an agreement on Wurk Happy, we send you and your client a pdf version of the agreement.

2. Manage work through your online agreement.

We send your client a daily digest of the tasks you've marked complete. If the scope of work changes, you can easily edit the agreement.

Regular client updates make the working relationship smoother, decreasing misunderstandings.

3. Get paid or pay through your online agreement.

When you request payment, we generate and send a PDF invoice to your client based on the tasks you have already marked complete

Because Wurk Happy provides you with your own merchant account, your client can pay you by credit card or bank account right through your online agreement.
