How to Use Wurk Happy

Simple Workflow

1. Create and send agreements in minutes

Our agreements are simple - just the scope of work and payment schedule so you and your client can focus on doing great work together.

When you create an agreement on Wurk Happy, we send you and your client a pdf version of the agreement.

2. Keep both parties on track

We update your client when you mark tasks complete.

Regular client updates make the working relationship smoother, decreasing misunderstandings.

You can also easily amend the agreement to adapt to changes in scope.

3. Get Paid

When you click 'request payment'. Your client receives a beautiful PDF invoice, automatically generated by the tasks you've marked complete.

When it's time for your client to pay, securely accept credit cards and bank transfers right through your agreement online.

Full Feature List

We make all this flexibility easy. Promise.

Agreement

Create and Send Simple Agreements
Create a beautiful, smart agreement in minutes. Our agreements organize the payment schedule and scope of work so you can focus on getting things done.
PDF & Online Access
When you create an agreement we send you and your client a PDF version. Agreements are managed through Wurkhappy.com so you can access and update your agreement from anywhere.
Edit agreements in progress
Ever been annoyed that you can't amend agreements to reflect changing business circumstances? Through Wurk Happy you can adjust the scope of work and payment terms with the approval of the other party - so the agreement always reflects the latest business requirements.
Freelancer or Client Flexibility
Through Wurk Happy you can create agreements as both the freelancer and the client. Need a little extra help on a project you got hired for. No problem. Manage it all through Wurk Happy.

Communication

Automatically Generated Invoices
Yep. Never create an invoice again. We pre-populate your invoices based on tasks and subtasks you made when creating the agreement. Invoices can be hourly or fixed. Don't worry though, you can change an agreement at any time.
Work Phases/milestones
When you create your agreement, you can group tasks into milestones or phases by adding subtasks. We do this automatically behind the scenes.
Real time status at a glance
We created a beautiful and simple status bar that reflects what work has been done and what payments have been made. All you need is a few seconds to know where everything stands.
Automatic Client Updates
Use Wurk Happy to check off work as you complete it. Make tasks as general or specific as you want.

Payment

Securely Process Credit Cards and Bank Transfers
When you accept your first payment through Wurk Happy, you create a merchant account through us that allows you to process credit cards and bank transfers directly into your bank account. We charge these payment methods at cost. Bank Tranfers: 1% up to $5 + 55 cents. Credit cards: 2.9% + 55 cents. When you receive payment we charge you 1% up to $10. Check out our pricing page for a clear overview.
Payment Schedules
Create payment schedules that let you and your client know the payment history and what payments need to be made next. Easily create schedules reflect the schedule of work or function completely independently
Payment upon agreement acceptance
Not sure about this new client? Require a deposit upon the acceptance of the agreement to protect yourself.
One Click Payment
When you receive or make a payment through Wurk Happy, you and your client's payment creditials are automatically remembered in our system so every future payment takes only one click.
