What is Wurk Happy?

Wurk Happy is an online platform that facilitates simple agreement, billing, and payment for freelancers and their clients.

We make the payment process fast and transparent by improving communication, reducing administration, and decreasing payment processing times.

Straightforward Pricing

Wurk Happy only charges a processing fee when you receive payment.

If you choose to get paid by bank transfer, we charge 1.5% of the payment received - up to a maximum fee per transaction of $15.

Funds are transferred from bank account to bank account, and are usually available within 1 business day.

If you choose to get paid by credit card, we charge 5% per transaction.

Funds are available within 5-7 business days (though it's often within 1-2 days) after transfer from your account with the payment processor.
